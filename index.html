About MonkeMayhem

MonkeMayhem is an innovative, hero-centric idle mobile RPG, designed for NFTs. It offers a unique use case for the existing NFT ecosystem. The game allows users to utilize their portfolio of NFTs as heroes, which can be used in a variety of gameplay scenarios, including dungeons, campaign explorations, player-versus-player combat, and large-scale community events. Heroes can level up and evolve, adding value to your on-chain assets and driving user engagement.

Think of the gameplay as similar to Raid Shadow Legends, but with the integration of blockchain technology and NFTs, providing a unique experience compared to traditional Web 2.0 games.

We aim to provide another use case for the SMB collections and extend support to other major Solana NFT collections to reach a wider audience!
